MSO Defined High Downforce Kit

Give your McLaren Линейка Sports uncompromising track poise. And take its performance to the next level. The MSO Defined High Downforce Kit from McLaren Special Operations (MSO) plants your car to the road with unshakeable stability.

The unmissable wing extends prominently from the rear, working the airflow to generate additional downforce. While behind the scenes – or, more accurately, under the floor – precisely engineered guide vanes filter the air that passes beneath the car to reduce drag.

 

Finished in Gloss Visual Carbon Fibre, the rear wing brings serious race aesthetics to your Линейка Sports. To each side, contrasting end plates featuring Silver Chromatic MSO branding – add a subtle touch. And supporting the wing, dynamic pylons are finished in High Gloss Black with Palladium Grey inserts.

 

Honed to perform

The High Downforce Kit is no mere decoration. Just like every single part of every single McLaren, it’s designed for pure purpose. In this case, to increase aerodynamic performance. The underfloor guide vanes are placed beneath the front of your Линейка Sports. They channel air away from the rear diffuser, forcing it to exit below the doors. This creates a low pressure zone at the front of the car, balancing the increased downforce from the rear wing. The High Downforce Kit adds an incredible 75kg of extra downforce – pushing your car’s surefootedness to even greater heights. This increases stability when traveling at speed, yet crucially leaves the characteristically agile dynamics of the Линейка Sports firmly intact during lower speed driving. The results open up heart-stopping new limits to your drive.

Looks that thrill

Carbon fibre. Its immense strength and supreme weight advantages were key to creating the High Downforce Kit. After all, the rear wing needs to withstand considerable air forces when travelling at speed – yet without slowing you down through additional weight. Finished in Gloss Visual Carbon Fibre, the rear wing brings serious race aesthetics to your Линейка Sports. To each side, contrasting end panels in Palladium Grey – featuring Silver Chromatic MSO branding – add a subtle touch. And supporting the wing, dynamic pylons are finished in High Gloss Black with Palladium Grey inserts. The wing is crowned by the central high-mounted brake light – a seamless upgrade to your Линейка Sports. One that complements its flowing, organic form in striking style.

Testing times

The Idiada proving ground, Spain. This demanding circuit is dedicated to McLaren development. From cars to components. This is where every one of our top secret innovations is put through its paces. The High Downforce Kit was no exception. The regimen was unforgiving. The kit had to endure 10,000 continuous miles of high speed usage. Measuring balance. Yaw gain. Performance metrics. All to ensure it would meet ultimate standards – in capability, and durability. Just like every McLaren Genuine Accessory.

Fit it to your Линейка Sports

The MSO High Downforce Kit is now available to be fitted to your existing 570S or 570S Spider model by your appointed McLaren Retailer. Simply get in touch to arrange at a time that suits you. Contact your McLaren Retailer for more information. Please note, the MSO High Downforce Kit is not compatible with the 570GT. Only available in selected markets. Please check with your Retailer.
